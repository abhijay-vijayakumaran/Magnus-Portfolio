ABOUT MAGNUS

Magnus Carlsen (born November 30, 1990, in Tønsberg) is a Norwegian chess grandmaster and former World Chess Champion. He won the World Chess Championship in 2013 by defeating Viswanathan Anand and successfully defended the title in 2014, 2016, and 2018. Carlsen is widely regarded as one of the greatest chess players of all time. Known for his strategic depth, exceptional endgame technique, and consistency across classical, rapid, and blitz formats, he has held the world’s No. 1 ranking for over a decade. His influence extends beyond competition, contributing significantly to the global popularity of modern chess.
